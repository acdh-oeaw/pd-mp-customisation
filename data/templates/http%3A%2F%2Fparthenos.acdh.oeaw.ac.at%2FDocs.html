Dataset

PARTHENOS Discovery gives access to the metadata records, contributed by its participating partners, which have been converted into a common semantic model, the PARTHENOS Entities Model.

Under Statistics you can get a broad overview of the overall dataset: Which sources contributed what information? How many instances are available per Class? How often are certain Properties used? etc.
